This document describes a vocabulary; in this case TertiaryEducationDiplomaEvidence-namespaces-voc-EN. A vocabulary is a collection of reusable terms, more precisely classes and properties, of which their semantics is fixed by means of a label and a definition. For terms definied in this domain additional formal agreements may be defined. The formal agremeents for external terms, reused in this domain, are adopted from that external source.
